Chilli Con Carne

Dilicious and easy to make chilli con carne recipes, Best served with rice, chedder cheese and uncle franks hot sauce if you need and extra kick!

Ingredients

    1 red onion
    2 cloves of garlic
    500g of minced beef
    1/2 tsp smoked paprika
    1/2 tsp paprike
    2 tsb cumin
    1tb chilli powder
    400g of kidney beans
    400g chopped tomatoes
    25g of dark chocolate

Steps

  1. Dice onion and garlic out in a pan on midium heat and cook for 10 minutes
  2. Take pan from heat and add spices, stir for 30 seconds
  3. Return pan to heat and add the minced beef, break up the beef and stir continuously until beef has browned
  4. Add tomato chopped tomatoes and kidney beans to pan and
  5. Leave to simmer for 1 hour adding water if the chilli becomes to dry
  6. Add dark chocolate and stir until fully dissolved into the chilli, leave for further ten minutes
  7. Take off the heat and serve!
