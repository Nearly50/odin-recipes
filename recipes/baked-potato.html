Simple and Cheap Meal, The Humble Baked Potato

The Humble baked potato, super simple super cheap and super scumptious!

Ingredients

  • A large potato, the bigger the better
  • salt for seasoning
  • 25g of salted butter
  • Tuna mayo, cottage cheese, cheeder cheese, and baked beans to accompany!

Steps

  1. Stab the potato with a fork on all sides
  2. Rub salt onto the potato and potato in the microwave and cook for 10 minutes, Preheat the over to 170C
  3. Put potato on a large baking tray and cook for 1 hour
  4. Remove from the over and, using a knife, put two perpendicular cuts about half way thorugh the potato
  5. Being careful not the burn yourself, use fingers and thumbs to push on each corner of the potato exposing the inside
  6. Add the butter, plate up with your chosen sides, and enjoy!
