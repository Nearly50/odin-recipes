Charizo Pasta

A simple and filling pasta dish with Spanish with a tomato and charizo sauce. Goes well with chedder cheese!

  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 of tomato passata
  • 200g of charizo
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 500g of dried penne pasta

    • Steps

    1. Dice onions and garlic and add to pan on medium heat and cook for 5 minutes
    2. Dice the charizo into 1cm cubes and add to the pan cook for 5 minutes
    3. Add passata to the pan and bring to a low simmer
    4. While the sauce cooks,
    5. Add pasta to a large saucepan and fill with water until the pasta is fully submerged
    6. When the pasta is cooked to your taste, drain of the water with a colender
    7. Add pasta and sauce back in the saucepan and stir until mixed evenly, then serve!
